ABOUT US

“Every successful project is a by-product of our hard work and determination gained by the trust and satisfaction of our customers”. SDC is evolved with more potential to acquire successful projects within cities as well for a rapid growth of the organization. Since 2022 SDC has been providing various services and helping people to fulfil their dream of construction. Head Quartered in Kurnool, SDC is one of the best construction companies with 2+ years of experience acquiring more than 75,000 customers. As of now, SDC has constructed an area of 50 lakh sq.ft which includes plots, apartments, gated communities, villas, commercial complexes and many more enticing projects. SDC has a legacy of building outstanding real estate projects in Kurnool, Anantapur, Nandyal, Tadipatri, and Kadapa in Andhra Pradesh, along with Ballari and Bengaluru in Karnataka, and Hyderabad in Telangana. It has new upcoming projects in Warangal and Nellore. SDC has also unfenced the barriers to commence a corporate office in Hyderabad soon for the reinforcement of the company’s goals.
